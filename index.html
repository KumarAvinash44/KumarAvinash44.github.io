About Me

Computer Vision Engineer and AI researcher with over 3 years of hands-on R&D experience, specializing in deep learning, generative models, and real-world image understanding tasks. My expertise spans from data preparation to designing and deploying state-of-the-art AI systems for image classification, segmentation, object detection, and image synthesis using GANs, diffusion models, and vision transformers.

My academic background includes an M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering, with a focus on computer vision and Generative AI. I conducted collaborative thesis research at ZHAW University in Switzerland alongside a team of biologists and aquaculture experts. This interdisciplinary experience sharpened my problem-solving, teamwork, and communication skills.

I am deeply passionate about bridging the gap between academic innovation and practical applications. Beyond technology, I thrive on exploration—whether it's traveling, learning languages, or immersing myself in new cultures and ideas. I believe growth comes from stepping outside your comfort zone and embracing diverse perspectives.
